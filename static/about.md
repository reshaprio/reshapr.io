## Mission

Making enterprise APIs AI-ready.

reShapr was born out of a hard truth: enterprises are under immense pressure to expose internal APIs to AI-native applications, but the path there is anything but smooth. Manual builds, fragile generators, and one-off integrations burn engineering time while introducing security risks and technical debt.

reShapr's objective: make enterprise API-to-MCP adoption fast, reliable, and secure. The platform instantly transforms existing APIs (REST, gRPC, GraphQL) into production-grade MCP servers — no code, no vendor lock-in, no compromises. Teams stay in control, deploy anywhere, and accelerate AI initiatives without rewriting anything.

reShapr is open source first: built in the open, shipped with transparency, with interoperability at the core so teams can adopt MCP without lock-in.
